Built for medical ward workflow

Every surface is tuned to the reality of a hospital shift.

Real-time notes

Post text notes with category + priority (Urgent, Normal, Low). Streamed live to every teammate in the ward.

Role-aware UI

Doctors create and manage. Nurses acknowledge. Admins run the house. Three flows, one app.

Multi-ward by design

Every ward has a unique ID. Share it, and anyone can join. Doctors run multiple wards at once — no switching.

Firebase-backed

Email/password, Google sign-in, and password reset baked in. Firestore security rules gate every action.

Notification panel

One tap on the bell surfaces every unacknowledged note, grouped by urgency. Ack in one click.

Light & dark

Material 3 theme with a dark mode tuned for fluorescent wards. Toggle from the top bar, remembered across sessions.

How it works

From zero to shared notes in 60 seconds.

  1. 1

    Sign up

    Email, password, role. Or continue with Google. Your account is private to you.

  2. 2

    Create or join a ward

    Start a ward — you get a short shareable ID. Paste it anywhere to invite teammates; they tap Join Ward and they're in.

  3. 3

    Post, see, acknowledge

    A note posted in the ward shows up in real time on every teammate's Home. Bells ring until someone acknowledges.
