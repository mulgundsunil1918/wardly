CUSTOM VISION LANGUAGE MODEL

Built different. Trained on real hospital data.

Not off-the-shelf OCR. A purpose-built Vision Language Model, fine-tuned on hundreds of real Indian hospital monitor photos across multiple brands and clinical scenarios.

🔌

Any monitor brand

Philips, GE, Mindray, Drager, BPL, Schiller — the AI reads them all. Different colors, layouts, label styles. No integration needed.

📷

Uses existing cameras

Works with your hospital's existing CCTV/IP cameras. No new hardware, no monitor-specific adapters, no HL7 gateway. Just point and read.

🫀

HR + PR independently

Reads Heart Rate (ECG) and Pulse Rate (SpO2 probe) as separate values — catches pulse-deficit, a real clinical finding most systems miss entirely.

🛡️

Safety-first design

The model is trained to say "I don't know" rather than guess. Blurry digits, disconnected sensors, alarm limits — all handled correctly. Wrong numbers don't reach the doctor.

🇮🇳

Made for Indian hospitals

Trained on photos from real Indian ICU/NICU wards. Handles the glare, angles, and mixed monitor brands that actually exist in Indian hospitals — not lab-perfect conditions.

⚡

Runs locally

The AI runs on a small box at the hospital — a Mac Mini or a refurbished PC with a GPU. No cloud dependency for vitals. Your patient data stays inside your hospital.

COMPLETE WARD PLATFORM

Not just monitoring — a connected ward

Wardly combines AI-powered vitals monitoring with real-time clinical notes, orders, and team communication. One app replaces the chaos of WhatsApp groups, paper notes, and phone tag.

📊

Live vitals dashboard

Every patient's HR, SpO2, RR, BP on one screen. Color-coded severity. Tap any patient for full detail view with trends and thresholds.

🚨

Auto-alerts with thresholds

Set custom alert thresholds per patient. Breach triggers push notifications to the whole ward team instantly. Critical vs. warning severity.

📝

Clinical orders & notes

Post orders and notes against specific patients. Visible on the bedside display. Normal, Urgent, or Low priority. Threaded replies with full audit trail.

✅

Acknowledgement system

See exactly who has seen and acted on every note. No more "did you get my message?" — timestamped, on the record.

📈

Vital trends over time

Interactive charts showing how each vital has changed over hours. Spot deterioration before it becomes an emergency.

🔒

Ward-private by design

Members of your ward see your ward — nothing else. No cross-ward data leakage. Built on Firebase with strict security rules.

GET STARTED

Up and running in minutes

No IT department needed. Any doctor or nurse can set up Wardly for their ward.

  1. 1

    Create your ward

    One person creates the ward — Wardly generates a 5-digit code. Share it with your team and they're in.

  2. 2

    Add your patients

    Add patients to the ward with bed number, diagnosis, and support details. Notes and vitals get filed against the right person.

  3. 3

    Connect a camera

    Point a phone or IP camera at the bedside monitor. The AI starts reading vitals immediately. Set alert thresholds per patient.

  4. 4

    Monitor from anywhere

    Live vitals on your phone, auto-alerts on breaches, post orders that appear at the bedside. The whole ward team stays in sync.
