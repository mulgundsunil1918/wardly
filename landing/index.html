THE PROBLEM

What goes wrong on a real ward

Every team has lived through these. Wardly was built around fixing each one.

💬

WhatsApp burial

Urgent updates buried under hundreds of unrelated messages in the ward group.

📝

Lost paper notes

Plans scribbled on a chart that nobody can find — or read — by handover.

❓

No ack trail

"Did you see the new plan?" "Did anyone act on it?" Nobody can prove it.

⏱

Phone tag

Nurse calls doctor, doctor calls back, patient waits. Minutes you don't have.

🔄

Broken handover

Outgoing team carries the context home in their head. Incoming team starts from zero.

👁

Can't watch every monitor

Nurses cover 4-6 patients. Vitals change while they're with another bed. Nobody sees it in time.

WARDLY — THE CORE

One live feed for the whole ward team

Wardly puts every patient note, plan, and alert in one shared timeline. Anyone on your ward — senior or junior, doctor or nurse — sees the same updates the moment they happen.

⚡

Real-time, every device

Post once — the whole team gets it instantly with a push notification. Works on phone, tablet, and browser.

✅

Acknowledged, on the record

See exactly who has seen and acted on every note. Timestamped. No more guessing.

📋

Orders & notes per patient

Post clinical orders and notes against specific patients. Normal, Urgent, or Low priority. Threaded replies.

🔒

Ward-private by design

Members of your ward see your ward — nothing more. No cross-ward data leakage.

🗂

Permanent patient history

Every note is filed against the patient, ready when handover hits. No more context lost between shifts.

👥

5-digit ward codes

Create a ward, share the code. Team members join in seconds. No IT department needed.

THE AI BEHIND WARDLY PRO

A custom Vision AI that reads any monitor

No proprietary hardware. No brand lock-in. Point your existing hospital camera at the bedside monitor — Wardly's custom-trained AI does the rest.

📹
1

Camera captures

Your existing IP/CCTV camera points at the bedside patient monitor. One camera covers 2-3 beds.

→
🧠
2

AI reads vitals

Our custom Vision Language Model extracts HR, SpO2, RR, BP from the screen — any brand, any layout.

→
📊
3

Live dashboard

Every patient's vitals update on your phone in real time. One screen, whole ward.

→
🚨
4

Auto-alerts

Thresholds breached? Push notification fires to the entire ward team instantly.

🔌

Any monitor brand

Philips, GE, Mindray, Drager, BPL, Schiller — the AI reads them all. Different colors, layouts, label styles. No integration needed.

📷

Uses existing cameras

Works with your hospital's existing CCTV/IP cameras. No new hardware, no monitor-specific adapters, no HL7 gateway.

🫀

HR + PR independently

Reads Heart Rate (ECG) and Pulse Rate (SpO2 probe) as separate values — catches pulse-deficit, a real clinical finding most systems miss.

🛡️

Safety-first design

The model is trained to say "I don't know" rather than guess. Blurry digits, disconnected sensors, alarm limits — all handled. Wrong numbers don't reach the doctor.

🇮🇳

Made for Indian hospitals

Trained on photos from real Indian ICU/NICU wards. Handles the glare, angles, and mixed monitor brands that actually exist here.

⚡

Runs locally

The AI runs on a small box at the hospital. No cloud dependency for vitals. Your patient data stays inside your hospital.
